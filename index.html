About Modern Borka Museum

Founded in 2024, Modern Borka Museum is India's first hybrid concept store — combining a physical museum of burqa heritage with an online premium garment store.

We believe modesty is not a limitation, it's an expression. Each burqa in our collection tells a story — from the royal courts of Hyderabad to the artisan workshops of Istanbul.
